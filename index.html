Current Signature Brews

Black Lodge Imperial Porter - 6.9% American Porter, 50 IBU

Black as midnight on a moonless night! This dark, roasty, smooth porter would do Agent Cooper proud. Slight hop bitterness and aroma, but dominated by coffee and chocolate notes. Be careful - too many sips could transport you in the Black Lodge.

Thai Fighter - 6.3% Specialty Ale, 49 IBU

This award-winning recipe is inspired by the flavors and ingredients of Thai food. We transform a pale ale with toasted coconut, peanut flour, and locally-grown Thai chilis. You read that right, this beer is a bit on the caliente side. If you brave the heat, you will be rewarded by a delicious mingling of interesting ingredients you won't normally find in a beer!

Halfling IPA - 4.9% India Pale Ale, 53 IBU

Hopped with the power combo of Columbus, Cascade, Citra, and Amarillo. An attractive golden hue, a citrusy aroma, a solid malty backbone, and the low gravity would keep any halfling coming back for another round.

Dalcassian - 4.8% Irish Red Ale, 21 IBU

A no-frills Irish Red brewed in honor of the Gaelic Irish tribe of the same name. Malty, slightly hoppy, refreshing. And red.
