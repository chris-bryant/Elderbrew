Oktoberfest 2016 Lineup

Damn Fine Coffee Stout

A thick, dark, full-bodied stout, with judicious amounts of caramel malt, chocolate malt, and flaked oats for a silky-smooth mouthfeel. This beer would be a stout-lover's dream as-is, but we kick it up a notch by cold-brewing Savage Gold coffee right in the fermenter before kegging, for a kick of coffee aroma and flavor that would make Agent Cooper proud.

ABV: 8% IBU: 60 SRM: ?

Thai Fighter Hot

A favorite at Oktoberfest and Thirsty Orange. We start off with a simple pale ale base with Motueka and Pacific Jade hops from New Zealand. After that, things get weird. We age this beer on powdered peanut, toasted coconut, Thai basil, and Thai chiles. Yeah, chiles. It's spicy. This strange brew works surprisingly well, and usually pours quick, so make it an early stop!

ABV: 6% IBU: 40 SRM: 30

Tart of Darkness New Brew

Tart of Darkness started out as a batch of To Everything There Is A Saison. After primary fermentation, we dumped in enough local blackberries to turn the beer purple and let it age for five months. The result is essentially liquid blackberry--impressively tart, purple, with a pink head. What else could you ask for?

ABV: 7.5% IBU: 20 SRM: ??? (it's purple)

Dad's NASCAR Beer New Brew

This is America--land of the free, home of corn beer. In honor of the timeless classic American pilsner, we decided to brew up our own take. This is the kind of beer your dad loved to drink on race day, but with a few Elderbrew twists to make it a little more enjoyable than a can of Steel Reserve.

ABV: 5% IBU: 25 SRM: 10
